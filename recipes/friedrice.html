Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meet. It is often eaten as a main dish or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 cup chopped white onion (1 small onion)
  • 1 cup frozen peas and carrots
  • 2-3 tablespoons soy sauce more or less to taste
  • 2 light beaten eggs
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat a large skillet or wok to medium heat. Add 2 tablespoons sesame oil, 1 cup chpped white onion, and 1 cup frozen peas and carrots. Cook until tender.
  2. Slide the vegatables to the side and pour 2 lightly beaten eggs onto the other side of the skillet. Using a spatula, scramble the eggs. Once cooked, mix the eggs with the vegetables.
  3. Add 3 cups cooked rize * to the veggie and egg mixture. Pour2-3 tablespoon soy sauce on top. Stir the rice and veggie mixture untile warmed through and combined.
  4. Top with 2 tablespoons chopped green onions, if desired, and serve immediately
